(About the Author)

Rashad Robinson has spent more than twenty years at the highest levels of leadership in two of the most significant social movements of our time.

He’s taken on tech giants and the criminal justice system, politicians in Washington and corporate executives in Hollywood. He’s been on the front lines of protest and in the back rooms of negotiation.

From Presence to Power is what he’s learned: a practical guide for anyone who wants to understand power, build it, and use it to win.

More information about Rashad, including his weekly How We Win newsletter, can be found at rashadrobinson.com.
